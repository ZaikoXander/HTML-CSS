We have horizontal tables:

By default, table headers are bold and centered:

Firstname Lastname Age
Jill Smith 50
Eve Jackson 94

But we can align to left:

Firstname Lastname Age
Jill Smith 50
Eve Jackson 94

Or we can have vertical tables:

Firstname Jill Eve
Lastname Smith Jackson
Age 94 50

Or have a Header for Multiple Columns:

Name Age
Jill Smith 50
Eve Jackson 94

Or add a caption as a heading for the entire table:

Montly savings
Month Savings
January $100
February $50

Or have a vertical Header for Multiple rows:

Name Jill
Phone 555-1234
